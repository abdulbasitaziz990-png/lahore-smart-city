About Lahore Smart City Watch

A GIS-based urban intelligence framework developed as a Final Year Project by students of Geographical Information System.

Our Mission

Lahore Smart City Watch aims to bridge the gap between citizens and urban management authorities through real-time geospatial intelligence. Our platform enables citizens to report infrastructure issues, track resolution progress, and access critical emergency services, all powered by GIS technology.

The Role of GIS

Geographic Information Systems (GIS) form the backbone of our platform. Every report is geotagged with precise coordinates, enabling spatial analysis of urban issues. This data-driven approach helps identify patterns, allocate resources efficiently, and make informed decisions for urban development and disaster management.
